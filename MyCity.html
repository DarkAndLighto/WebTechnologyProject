Al Rahman Mosque

Al-Rahman Mosque or Al-Rahman Mosque is located on King Faisal Street in the Syrian city of Aleppo. This mosque is distinguished by its distinctive construction method and is one of the unique mosques in construction. It is a mosque built according to modern systems on ancient Islamic architectural foundations dating back to the first Islamic eras, and this is indicated by the square minarets with quadrilateral sides.
